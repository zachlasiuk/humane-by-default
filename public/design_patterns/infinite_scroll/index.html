Infinite Scroll

User Goal: Consume information

Business Objective: Increase engagement

Harmful or Humane: Harmful

Values: Agency

Explanation:

Why It Matters: Users lose track of time, increases anxiety. INCENTIVISES ADDICTIVE BEHAVIOR.

Examples: Social media feeds that scroll, and scroll, and scroll.

How to Avoid: Ask yourself, what do you want to do right now? And set app timers.

Alternative Designs: Infinite Scroll with Checkins Paged Content
