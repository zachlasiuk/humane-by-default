Infinite Scroll with Checkins

User Goal: Consume information

Business Objective: Increase engagement

Harmful or Humane: Humane

Values: Agency

Explanation:

Why It Matters: Creates positive friction, with a specific decision point for the user interrupting flow. Actually increases MEANINGFUL engagement, rousing people from their stupor to pay more attention.

Examples: Netflix's "Are you still watching?" check-ins. TikTok's digital wellbeing prompts after extended scrolling.

How to Avoid:

Alternative Designs: Infinite Scroll Paged Content
